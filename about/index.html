About

1001-01-01
Hello! My name is Haoran Xu. I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Stanford University. My advisor is Fredrik Kjolstad. Prior to that, I spent my undergraduate years at MIT, during which I was privileged to have worked with Vladimir Kiriansky, Saman Amarasinghe, Richard Peng, and many others. I also had a short but wonderful experience working as a database developer at MemSQL.

This blog consists mostly of my random notes. It is my great pleasure if any of my posts are helpful to you.

The title of this blog is a quote from 120 Yen Stories: Winter, one of my favourite stories by Kataoka Tomo. The footer picture is from Girls’ Last Tour, a great manga by Tsukumizu.
